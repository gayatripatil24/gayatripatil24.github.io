About us

Clean Science and Technology Limited [NSE: CLEAN; BSE: 543318] delivers innovative and sustainable solutions with unique performance ingredients, meeting the demands of both consumer and industrial markets globally. Incorporated in 2006, we are a fine and specialty chemical manufacturing company with unique and innovative catalytic chemistries. Clean Science and Technology Ltd. focuses on developing clean and eco-friendly manufacturing processes. We take pride in being world market leaders with a strong global presence. Clean Fino-Chem Limited is a 100% owned subsidiary of Clean Science and Technology Ltd., incorporated in 2022. CFCL is India's first Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS) manufacturing facility. Visit us at www.cleanfinochem.com to learn more about our innovative solutions and offerings.
